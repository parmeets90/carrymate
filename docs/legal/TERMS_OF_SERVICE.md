## 1. What CarryMate is — and is not
CarryMate is a **technology marketplace** that connects people who want to send personal items ("Senders") with verified air travellers who have spare baggage capacity on a route ("Travellers"), and provides identity verification, in-app messaging, an escrow-style payment hold, and dispute tools.

**CarryMate is NOT:**
- a courier, freight forwarder, shipping line, or logistics carrier;
- a customs broker or import agent;
- a bank, money transmitter, or escrow agent (payment and fund-holding are performed by our licensed payment partner, see §7);
- a party to the actual carriage agreement between a Sender and a Traveller.

The carriage arrangement is **directly between the Sender and the Traveller.** CarryMate provides the marketplace and tools only.

## 2. Eligibility
You must be **at least 18 years old**, legally able to enter contracts, and complete identity verification (KYC) before you transact. You agree to provide accurate information and keep it current. ## 3. Identity verification (KYC)
KYC is **mandatory before any transaction.** Verification is performed via our verification partner (currently Didit) and/or manual review (Aadhaar/PAN for Senders, passport for Travellers, plus a liveness selfie). You consent to this processing as described in the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md). You must not impersonate any person or use another's identity documents.

You are responsible for any loss, penalty, or seizure arising from a misdescribed, prohibited, or undeclared item you send.

## 5. Traveller responsibilities — importer of record & customs

If you are unsure whether an item is permissible, **do not carry it.**

## 7. Payments, escrow & fees
- When a Sender accepts a Traveller's bid, the Sender pays the carry fee into a **hold** managed by our **licensed payment partner**. Funds are **released to the Traveller only after delivery is confirmed** (by the Sender, or by auto-confirmation after the defined window).
- CarryMate charges a **platform commission of [15]%** of the carry fee. Fees are shown before you confirm.
- **Refunds** (full or partial) are issued for cancellations and dispute outcomes per §9. CarryMate does not hold or lend funds; all money movement is performed by the payment partner.
- All amounts are in **Indian Rupees (₹)** during the pilot.

Decisions are made in good faith on the evidence; you agree to cooperate and provide truthful information.
