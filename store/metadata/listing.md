# CarryMate — App Store Listing Metadata (Challenge 07)

Framing rule: CarryMate is a **personal errand / community marketplace**, NOT a
courier or logistics product. Never use: ship, courier, freight, logistics,
delivery service, cross-border delivery, send parcels, international shipping,
cargo. Prefer: "connect with a traveler", "trusted community member carrying",
"peer-to-peer personal item sharing", "homemade food and personal gifts".

## Category
- Google Play: **Lifestyle** (not Business, not Shopping)
- App Store: **Lifestyle** (Primary), Travel (Secondary)

## Title
CarryMate: Send with Travelers

## Short description (Play, ≤80 chars)
Connect with trusted travelers to carry homemade food & gifts between India & UAE.

## Keywords (App Store, ≤100 chars)
NRI community, India UAE, trusted travelers, personal gifts, homemade food, send with traveler

## Full description
CarryMate connects people in the India–UAE community with trusted travelers
already flying their route, so you can send homemade food, sweets, sarees,
documents and festival gifts to family and friends.

Every traveler is identity-verified, every payment is held safely in escrow and
released only after the recipient confirms, and every handover starts with an
open-box check — so both sides feel safe.

• Connect with verified community members flying your route
• Payments protected in escrow until hand-off is confirmed
• Open-box check + photo records on every item
• In-app chat with contact details kept private
• Ratings and trust badges on every profile

CarryMate is a technology platform that connects individuals. It is not a
courier company, freight forwarder, or customs agent. Travelers carry items as
personal luggage and are responsible for what they accept. Personal items only —
no electronics, medicines, liquids, commercial goods, or items over ₹10,000.
